what is computer privacy

Over the years, computers have been constantly changing our daily lives such as buying items online or just simply browsing or playing games. These are the things that invade our privacy,whether it be researching for an assignment or just simply fooling around on the internet, people are always at risk. Unfortunately, ever since computers were engineered and the internet was invented, people's privacy on the computer has always been an issue. Problems such as personal information being leaked on the internet due to viruses, hackers, or online services selling their clients information to marketers for money, lead to the fact that being on the internet is a huge risk concluding the fact that people’s private information can be easily exposed.

Example of privacy invasion

    Being on the internet is a risky thing, such as going on popular social media sites such as Facebook. There has been cases of people stalking others on facebook and finding out their personal information such as family members, home addresses, and phone numbers. Examples like these provide enough information that people need to take precautions in order to keep their private information from other people.
